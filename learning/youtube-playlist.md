## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Don't Binge Watch** - Watch one video, build something, then move on
2. **Speed is OK** - 1.25-1.5x speed for familiar concepts
3. **Pause & Code** - Stop video and try implementing before they show solution
4. **Take Notes** - Use `learning/notes/` folder
5. **Join Communities** - Share progress in Discord/Reddit
6. **Rewatch** - Second viewing often reveals missed details
